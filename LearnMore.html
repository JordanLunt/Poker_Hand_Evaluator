Poker hands from highest to lowest

Ranking Hand

Example

1.Royal Flush

AC, KC, QC, JC, 10C: All OfThe Same Suit

2.Straight Flush

1C,2C,3C,4C,5C: Five cards in a sequence, all in the same suit

3.Four of a kind

1C,1D,1H,1S,2C: All four cards of the same rank

4.Full house

1C,1D,1H,2C,2D: Three of a kind with a pair

5.Flush

1C,3C,5C,7C,9C: Any five cards of the same suit, but not in a sequence

6.Straight

1C,2S,3C,4D,5C: Five cards in a sequence, but not of the same suit

7.Three of a kind

1C,1D,1H,2H,5D: Three cards of the same rank

8.Two pair

1C,1D,2C,2D,4H: Two different pairs

9.Pair

1C,1D,2D,3D,4H: Two cards of the same rank

10.No Hand

There is no hand
